Data Deletion Request

This page handles data deletion requests for users who logged in via Facebook.

Request Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your data associated with social login, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at privacy@wasserman.me
  2. Include the following information:
    • Your name
    • Email address used for social login
    • Social platform used (Google/Facebook)
    • Reason for deletion request
  3. We will process your request within 30 days

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your social login connection
  • Associated profile information from social platforms
  • Your user account (if requested)

What Data Will Be Retained

Important: Flight training records are required to be retained for 7 years under aviation regulations and cannot be deleted unless legally required.

Alternative Options

Instead of full deletion, you can:

  • Disconnect social login: Remove the link between your account and social platforms
  • Deactivate account: Keep records but disable login access
  • Update preferences: Modify what information is shared
Request Data Deletion Privacy Policy

This process complies with GDPR, CCPA, and Meta's data deletion requirements.
