Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ "now"|date:"F d, Y" }}

1. Information We Collect

{{ CLUB_NAME }} ("we," "us," or "our") operates a flight training management system. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, email address, full name
  • Flight Records: Flight hours, dates, instructor notes, training progress
  • Personal Details: License numbers, medical certificate information

1.2 Social Login Information

When you log in using Google or Facebook, we only collect:

  • Your email address (to match with existing accounts)
  • Your name and profile information
  • We do NOT store your social media access tokens
  • We do NOT access your social media posts or contacts

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Flight Training Management: Track your progress, schedule flights, maintain records
  • Safety and Compliance: Maintain required aviation training records
  • Communication: Send important updates about your training
  • Account Security: Verify your identity and protect your account

3. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Aviation Authorities: When required by law for safety compliance
  • Instructors: Your assigned flight instructors can view your training records
  • Club Management: Authorized club officials for operational purposes
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure database storage
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and user authentication
  • Regular backups with encryption

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

6. Data Retention

Flight training records are retained for a minimum of 7 years as required by aviation regulations. Account information is retained while your account is active, or as required by law.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for:

  • User authentication and session management
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • System functionality

We do NOT use tracking cookies for advertising or analytics.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: For optional social login (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Facebook Login: For optional social login (governed by Meta's Privacy Policy)
  • Cloud Hosting: Secure cloud infrastructure for data storage

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. Flight training typically requires participants to be at least 16 years old.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes via email or system notification.
